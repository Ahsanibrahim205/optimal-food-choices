Carbohydrates

  • Carbohydrates are the main source of energy for the body and for the brain. There are 2 major types:
    1. Simple carbohydrates (also called simple sugars) found in fruits, milk and refined and unrefined sugar
    1. Complex carbohydrates(starches) found in starchy vegetables, bread, pasta, crackers, cereals, pulses, rice and other grains
  • How much carbohydrate is needed? It is usually advised that carbohydrates make up 50-60% of your diet. Of these 80% are best chosen as complex carbohydrates, which are high in fibre.
